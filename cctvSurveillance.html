CCTV SURVEILLANCE

CCTV (close circuit television) has become more popular in the last several years thanks to lower cost equipment becoming available. CCTV is also an excellent way to monitor and ensure the safety of your location..

There are two popular forms of CCTV systems:

Analog->These systems run over traditional coax cable that must be run through the walls and/or ceilings. The cameras connect back to a central recorder. Video quality is analog.


IP Camera Systems->These are the next generation of CCTV systems. They run over Cat5/5e/6 network cable. All video is recorded in high definition to a digital video recorder (DVR).
